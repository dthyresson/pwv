PWV invests in early-stage technology companies.

PWV is a team of three entrepreneurs and technologists: Tom Preston-Werner (GitHub cofounder), David S. Price, and A. David Thyresson. Beyond capital, we leverage our unparalleled network and expertise to provide ongoing support, helping startups achieve product-market fit faster and scale effectively with the right growth partners.

Founders want us on their cap table

We build relationships that deliver unmatched value and are sought after by founders and referred by top-tier co-investors for our expertise.

With deep experience and a proven track record of scaling transformative companies, PWV doesn’t just invest—we amplify— leveraging our network to unlock exponential opportunities.

Our Values

We are committed to a vision of a future where technological progress and human flourishing go hand in hand. And we invest to help make this future possible.

Our Approach

Backed by a track record of early investments in today’s most iconic brands and deep operational know-how, we are committed to identifying and empowering the next generation of industry leaders.

Our strategy balances disciplined valuations, diversified risk, and targeted follow-ons, leveraging the fund’s reputation, network, and expertise to deliver outsized returns.

  • Early Stage: We make meaningful equity investments in early-stage companies.
  • Exceptional Opportunities: We invest in breakout winners and exceptional opportunities.
  • Follow-on: We support growth with additional capital.
